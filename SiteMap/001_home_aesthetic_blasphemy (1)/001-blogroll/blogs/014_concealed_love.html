What should I say to make your emotions to swell?
To break this wall of silence that stands between,
Should I play the gambit of Forever Goodbyes?
Or should I say nothing, just walk away?

What should I say that only you would know?
Like a sign, that what I want is you,
Emotions, that were contained and buried,
Now ready to bear butterfly wings, and fly to you.

As through this prose, I offer you my rose,
Its refuses to bloom, till it finds you near,
On my knees, I implore to thee, my majesty
Would this be enough to be a sincere propose?

I have allowed you in, in somewhere deep,
A place, not everyone is allowed to peek
And I promise you, that I’ll love you,
As I have, yesterday, today and forever.
